The Icalier Career Path

For several days we have analysed different elements considered by technical and creative people in the digital industry. We believe more options are going to be added in the near future, but so far this is a path that can lead you to learn and grow in a constant, unstoppable but most importantly, human way.


Every single memeber in our team has a monetary and emotional remuneration, sustained by the knowledge and experiences he or she gets. Along the Icalier life, every 3 months we celebrate a team evaluation to assess new developed capabilities and challenges faced.

Perks for the team

We care about our team. We offer different perks to motivate and encourage the constant development of our passions and professional skills. These elements are additional to any basic offering as a part-time or full-time team member.

Join our family!

If you want to live a different work experience, develop your true passions and grow as an individual, join us in this adventure. Let us know you want to be an Icalier.
